About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Linguistics at the University of Arizona. My research focuses on the syntax-morphology interface, particularly in Iranian languages. I am interested in alignment systems, case, and agreement.

This website serves as a central hub for my research, publications, and professional activities. Feel free to explore my work and get in touch.
